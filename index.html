The Spy

He is a puzzle, wrapped in an enigma, shrouded
in riddles, lovingly sprinkled with intrigue, express
mailed to Mystery, Alaska, and LOOK OUT
BEHIND YOU! but it is too late. You're dead.
For he is the Spy - globetrotting rogue, lady killer
(metaphorically) and mankiller (for real).
